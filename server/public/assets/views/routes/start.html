You will be presented with a choice of class and sub-section.
Get ready to play, as you only have 60 seconds to earn a point for each question answered correctly! When a flash card is presented, choose carefully to select the right answer. If you get it right, you earn 1 point. Get it wrong, and you can move on to the next question.
When you are finished with all of the questions or your time runs out, you can play another class to earn more points. Good luck!
